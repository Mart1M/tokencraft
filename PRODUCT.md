# Product

## Register

Product

## Platform

web

## Users

Design system teams who create, maintain, and share the design tokens that keep their products consistent.

## Product purpose

TokenCraft is a local-first editor for design token JSON. It helps teams create and manage their design system tokens while keeping the source files in their own project.

## Positioning

An open-source, local-first design token editor: no hosted service is required, and the JSON remains owned and controlled by the team.

## Brand personality

Efficient, flexible, open source.

## Anti-reference

Avoid a generic, indistinguishable dashboard with no deliberate product character.

## Design principles

- Keep the JSON source of truth close, understandable, and in the team's control.
- Make complex token work quick to scan, edit, validate, and review.
- Support adaptable workflows instead of imposing rigid SaaS conventions.
- Give expert teams clear state, reliable local control, and safe changes.
- Make the product's openness and transparency visible in its behavior and language.
